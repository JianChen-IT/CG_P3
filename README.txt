To use the application, we followed the proposed interaction in the labs:

- Left key: move eye to the left
- Right key: move eye to the right
- Up key: move eye upwards
- Down key: move eye downwards

- A key: move center to the left
- S key: move center to downwards
- D key: move center to the right
- W key: move center upwards

- F key: decreasing the field of view
- G key: increasing the field of view

Additional interactions:

- Press 1: to only have the object painted with lines (no z-buffer implemented here)
- Press 2: to have the interpolated mode
- Press 3: to have the texture mode
